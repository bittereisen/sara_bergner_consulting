About Sara

At Bergner Business Consulting, Sara specializes in working with small businesses to help them grow and thrive. Sara is currently offering free consultations as she builds her practice. Her goal is to help your team succeed by providing personalized strategies and solutions without any cost to you. Sara takes the time to speak with all employees and learns each person’s job to better understand the company and find the best solutions for growth.

Sara prides herself on honesty, fairness, and a reputation for saying what others might be afraid to say. With a diverse background in various roles, she has helped businesses improve their operations, streamline their functions, and increase revenue in each position she’s held.
