Our Services and Fee Structure

At Sara Bergner Business Consulting, we offer a range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of your business. Below is an overview of our offerings and associated fees:

Initial Consultation

Duration: 1 hour

Fee: $100

Description: An overview of our services, understanding your business needs, and outlining potential strategies for growth.

Comprehensive Business Assessment

Duration: 3-5 hours

Fee: $300 to $500

Description: An in-depth analysis of your current business operations, identifying strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.

Strategy Development and Implementation Planning

Duration: 5-8 hours

Fee: $500 to $800

Description: Crafting tailored strategies and actionable plans designed to foster business growth and operational efficiency.

Ongoing Support and Coaching

Duration: Monthly sessions

Fee: $150 per hour

Description: Regular check-ins to assess progress, evaluate outcomes, and adjust strategies as necessary to ensure sustained growth.

Note: Fees are subject to change based on the specific needs and size of your business. For a personalized quote or to schedule a consultation, please contact us.
